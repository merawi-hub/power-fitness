About POWERFIT

Our mission is to empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals in a supportive and motivating environment.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, PowerFit began with a simple vision: to create a fitness center that caters to individuals of all fitness levels and backgrounds. What started as a small gym with limited equipment has grown into a state-of-the-art facility with multiple locations across the country.

Our founder, Hintsa Ambaw, a former professional athlete, recognized the need for a gym that combined premium equipment with expert guidance and a supportive community. This philosophy remains at the core of everything we do at PowerFit.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of members transform their lives through fitness, and we continue to evolve and improve our offerings to meet the changing needs of our community.
