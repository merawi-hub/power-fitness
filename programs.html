Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need prior experience with strength training?

    No prior experience is necessary. Our program is designed for all fitness levels, and our trainers will guide you through proper form and technique from day one. We'll start with an assessment to determine your current fitness level and customize the program accordingly.

  • What equipment will I be using?

    You'll have access to a variety of equipment including barbells, dumbbells, kettlebells, squat racks, bench press stations, cable machines, and more. Our facility is fully equipped with professional-grade equipment to ensure safe and effective workouts.

  • How many sessions per week are recommended?

    For optimal results, we recommend attending 3 sessions per week with rest days in between. This frequency allows for proper recovery while still providing enough stimulus for strength gains. However, the program can be adjusted based on your schedule and recovery capacity.
